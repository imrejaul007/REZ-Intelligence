# Cosmic OS - Complete Feature Specification

**"AI-Powered Human Life Intelligence OS"**

---

## Executive Summary

Cosmic OS transforms raw behavioral data from the REZ ecosystem into **symbolic, abstracted insights** through an AI Council of specialized agents. It provides wellness rewards, daily guidance, and life wisdom without feeling like surveillance.

**Key Differentiator:**
> Users should NEVER feel "This company tracks everything."  
> Instead they should feel "This ecosystem understands me."

## 6. PRIVACY & TRUST LAYER

### 6.1 Core Principles

| Instead of... | We say... |
|--------------|-----------|
| "Traveled 4 times" | "Fresh environments may inspire" |
| "Spent ₹50,000" | "Abundance flows when aligned" |
| "Slept 5 hours" | "Rest supports your energy" |
| "Argued with partner" | "Relationships need nurturing" |
| "No exercise this week" | "Movement invites vitality" |

### 6.2 Abstraction Rules
1. **NEVER surface raw data** - Always abstract
2. **Use symbolic language** - Cosmic metaphors
3. **Positive framing** - Growth-oriented
4. **Timing over certainty** - "May" not "Did"
5. **User benefit focus** - Help, not monitor

### 6.3 Consent & Transparency
- **Opt-in mood tracking**
- **Clear data usage explanation**
- **User control over signals**
- **Privacy-first defaults**
