## FAQ

### How are API calls counted?
Each API request to our endpoints counts as one API call. Batch endpoints count each item separately. Health checks and authentication don't count.

### What happens if I exceed my limit?
You'll receive an alert at 80% usage. At 100%, additional requests are queued or rejected based on your plan. You can purchase add-ons or upgrade anytime.

### Can I change plans?
Yes, upgrade anytime (immediate effect). Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, 14-day trial with Professional features. No credit card required.

### How does annual billing work?
Annual billing gives you 2 months free (17% savings). Payment upfront for the year.
